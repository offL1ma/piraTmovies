Call me by your name

Synopsis

"Call Me by Your Name" is a 2017 romantic drama film directed by Luca Guadagnino and starring Timothée Chalamet and Armie Hammer. The story takes place in 1983 in Italy and revolves around Elio Perlman, an intelligent, cultured, and sensitive young man who falls in love with Oliver, an American scholar working with Elio’s father during the summer. The film sensitively and intricately portrays Elio’s discovery of his sexuality and his relationship with Oliver, serving as a meditation on love, loss, and the passage of time. The film was critically acclaimed and received numerous awards.
