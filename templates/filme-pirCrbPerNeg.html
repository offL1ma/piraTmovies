Pirates of the Caribbean: The curse of the black pearl

Synopsis

"Pirates of the Caribbean: The Curse of the Black Pearl" is a 2003 adventure and fantasy film directed by Gore Verbinski and starring Johnny Depp, Orlando Bloom, and Keira Knightley. The film follows Captain Jack Sparrow, an eccentric pirate seeking to reclaim the Black Pearl, a ship stolen by his former partner Barbossa. The movie is remembered for its thrilling action, quirky humor, and Johnny Depp’s iconic performance as Jack Sparrow.
