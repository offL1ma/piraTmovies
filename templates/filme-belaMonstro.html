The Beauty and the Beast

Synopsis

"Beauty and the Beast" is a live-action adaptation of Disney’s animated classic, released in 2017 and directed by Bill Condon. The film tells the story of Belle, an intelligent and independent young woman who becomes the prisoner of a Beast in his enchanted castle. Over time, Belle discovers that behind the Beast’s frightening appearance lies a cursed prince, and that true love can break the spell. With stunning sets, a memorable soundtrack, and a deeper approach to the characters, the film brings back with emotion and magic one of the most beloved fairy tales of all time.
