Corpse Bride

Synopsis

"Corpse Bride" is a dark yet charming animated film directed by Tim Burton and Mike Johnson, released in 2005. The story follows Victor, a shy young man who, while rehearsing his wedding vows in the forest, accidentally becomes betrothed to Emily — a corpse bride who takes him to the land of the dead. As he tries to return to the world of the living to marry his true fiancée, Victoria, Victor finds himself torn between two worlds and two loves. With a gothic aesthetic, a striking soundtrack, and a sensitive narrative, the film blends romance, humor, and fantasy in a story about love, loss, and redemption.
