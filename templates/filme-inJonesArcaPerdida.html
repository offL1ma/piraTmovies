Indiana Jones - Raiders of the lost Ark

Synopsis

"Raiders of the Lost Ark" is an adventure film directed by Steven Spielberg and released in 1981, starring Harrison Ford as the archaeologist Indiana Jones. The plot follows Jones as he is hired by the United States government to find the Ark of the Covenant, which is said to contain the Ten Commandments from the Bible. Throughout his quest, he faces dangers and obstacles and is accompanied by his former girlfriend, Marion Ravenwood. The film is remembered for its thrilling action scenes, innovative special effects, and iconic soundtrack, and is considered a classic of adventure cinema and one of Spielberg’s most popular films.
