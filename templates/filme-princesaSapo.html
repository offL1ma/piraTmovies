The Princess and the frog

Synopsis

"The Princess and the Frog" is a Disney musical animation released in 2009 and directed by Ron Clements and John Musker. Set in the vibrant city of New Orleans, the film tells the story of Tiana, a hardworking and determined young woman who dreams of opening her own restaurant. Her life changes drastically when she meets Prince Naveen, who has been turned into a frog by a curse. When she kisses him, Tiana also transforms into a frog, and together they embark on an adventure through the bayou to break the spell. With a jazz-inspired soundtrack, charismatic characters, and a powerful message about hard work and perseverance, the film offers a magical reimagining of a classic fairy tale.
