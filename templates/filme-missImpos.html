Mission: Impossible

Synopsis

"Mission: Impossible" is a 1996 action film directed by Brian De Palma and starring Tom Cruise. The story follows Ethan Hunt, an IMF secret agent who is accused of betraying his team and embarks on a dangerous mission to clear his name and find the real traitor. The film is remembered for its thrilling action scenes and intriguing mystery plot, and it was a major box-office success that launched a successful franchise.
