Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone

Synopsis

“Harry Potter and the Philosopher’s Stone” is a 2001 fantasy film based on the novel of the same name by J.K. Rowling. It tells the story of Harry Potter, a young orphan who discovers he is a wizard and is invited to attend Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. There, he befriends Ron Weasley and Hermione Granger, and together they investigate a mystery involving the Philosopher’s Stone, a valuable and dangerous magical object sought by Voldemort. The film was a major box-office success and launched one of the most successful franchises in cinema history.
