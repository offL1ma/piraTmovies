The Platform

Synopsis

"The Platform" is a science fiction and suspense film set in a vertical prison tower where inmates are fed via a platform that descends floor by floor. Goreng, the protagonist, struggles to survive the cruel and inhumane system while befriending his cellmate, Trimagasi. As the plot unfolds, the audience is confronted with questions about human nature, morality, and social inequality. The ending is left open to the viewer’s interpretation.
